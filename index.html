About the Festival

The Festival of Wealth (FOW) is a transformative one-day event designed to equip recent graduates with the tools, networks, and mentorship they need to build lasting financial stability and accelerate their professional growth.

Held on October 18th, 2025, the festival will gather 1,000–2,000 bright young minds under the theme “Wealth in Knowledge, Networks, and Opportunities.”

Attendees Will Experience:

  • Inspiring keynote sessions from renowned speakers.
  • Engaging panel discussions on career growth.
  • Exclusive mentorship opportunities with industry leaders.
  • Open networking sessions for lasting connections.
  • A platform where bright minds meet big opportunities — helping graduates unlock their potential and step confidently into the future.
