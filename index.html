About the Festival

The Festival of Wealth (FOW) is a transformative one-day event designed to equip recent university graduates with the tools, networks, and mentorship they need to build lasting financial stability and accelerate their professional growth.
Held on October 18th, 2025, the festival will gather 1,000–2,000 bright young minds under the theme “Wealth in Knowledge, Networks, and Opportunities.”

Attendees will experience:

  • Inspiring keynote sessions from 3–5 renowned speakers sharing real-world insights.
  • Engaging panel discussions offering multi-perspective dialogue on career growth and financial empowerment.
  • Exclusive mentorship slots for standout participants to connect directly with industry leaders.
  • Open networking sessions to build meaningful peer and mentor relationships.
  • The Festival of Wealth is where bright minds meet big opportunities — helping graduates unlock their potential and step confidently into the future.
